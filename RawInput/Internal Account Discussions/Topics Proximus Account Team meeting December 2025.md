Cloudera and MIT Migration Progress and Product Team Dependencies:

	MIT Refactoring Challenges: Daniel explained that the MIT refactoring migration is hindered by instability in the customer environment, leading users to avoid UAT and integration testing, which impacts milestone invoicing; negotiations are ongoing to have the milestone recognized despite these external issues.
